About Me

I recently finished my B.A. in chemistry at Boston University ,and I am currently working at Snapdragon Chemistry, Inc. across the Charles River in Cambridge. My research interests lie primarily in catalytic reaction methodology development and natural product synthesis.

While at BU, I worked with myresearch advisor, Jim Panek, on developing novel catalytic reactions enhanced by the unique electronic properties of organosilane reagents.

At Snapdragon, I have become familiar with performing a wide range of organic reactions in batch as well as continuous flow. During the course of my work there, I have developed an interest in designing photochemical and photoredox catalyzed reactions.

Thank you for taking a look at my site, and please feel free to contact me with questions or comments.
